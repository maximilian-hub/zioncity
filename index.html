We Are

More than just a band--we are a musical journey. Our distinct fusion of jazz, blues, rock, and the boundless energy of our beloved Big Easy, resonates in every note we play and has become the lifeblood of our electrifying performances.

The story of how we met is nothing short of extraordinary. One fateful night during the renowned Mardi Gras festival, each of us, unbeknownst to the others, was following a mystical melody that seemed to waft through the air. It led us to an old, hidden jazz club, where a crowd was gathered around a 100-year-old piano said to be played by the ghosts of legendary musicians. As the clock struck midnight, the piano came to life, playing a captivating tune. As if drawn by an unseen force, we each picked up an instrument and began to play along. The room was electrified, and we knew we had stumbled upon something magical. The spirits of New Orleans had brought us together, and Zion City was born.

When the global pandemic brought the world to its knees, shutting down venues and quieting the once-lively streets, we refused to let the music die. Where others saw the end, we saw an opportunity. Zion City turned front porches into our stages and ordinary nights into unforgettable musical celebrations, breathing life back into the city.

But our commitment goes beyond the melodies and rhythms. We realized that our community needed sustenance for both body and soul. With every porch performance, we organized food drives, combining the euphoria of our music with a heartfelt gesture of solidarity and care for our neighbors.

We take immense pride in our professionalism and the quality of our performances, but it's the principles that guide us that define who we are. Here at Zion City, it’s not just about the music – it’s about making a real impact. Our dedication, hard work, and passion for positive change make us more than musicians; they make us stewards of the undying spirit of New Orleans.

Join us on this remarkable journey as we continue to use our music as a force for unity, strength, and inspiration. With Zion City, every note is a piece of our heart, and every performance is an invitation to be part of something bigger. We are the heartbeat of New Orleans, and we're here to stay.
